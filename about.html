Purpose

Goal of The Spore website is to announce to the public and sell used items from the University of Wisconsin Eau Claire. The website’s focus will provide customers information notifying weekly, specifically Thursdays, of the used items that will be on sale and available to the public. The public will be made aware of The Spore through the website. Of course, as well as pricing, location, and contact information, these will be made available for easy access.

History

The Spore is made based on the foundation of UW Eau Claire’s surplus store. This has been made available to students and locals which offer them a great deal in used items. The Spore branches with UW Eau Claire’s surplus store but The Spore offers easy accessibility via the web and promotes what UW Eau Claire has to offer which is affordable.
